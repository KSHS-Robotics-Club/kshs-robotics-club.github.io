About Us

KSHS has a variety of robotics programs spanning across all grades. Along with robotics classes, we enter teams into a variety of FIRST competitions such as FIRST Lego League and FIRST Robotics Competition.

FLL

KSHS first competed in FLL in 2018 but after 2019 went dormant for multiple years. In 2023, KSHS entered a team for the first time in 4 years, comprised of a group of year 8s and 9s. This year we have started our preparation early and are looking to send multiple teams with students ranging from years 7 to 9.

FRC

This year we also plan to send a team to WARP to compete in the 2024 FIRST Robotics competition game. The team will be comprised of older students, allowing FLL students to transition into it after graduating from the FLL program.
